Explore Community Energy

Imagine a neighbourhood where efficient homes generate energy and share it with the community. Imagine a walkable community, with local services and convenient access to transit in a region that uses renewable energy resources instead of fossil fuels.


The Community Energy Explorer (CEE) is a web resource, designed to provide communities and citizens with information and tools for local energy planning and carbon emission mitigation. The long term goals of CEE are to provide a platform for evolving and up-to-date resources for community energy planning, and the development of new and innovative engagement tools around energy.
